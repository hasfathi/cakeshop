ABOUT US

Cake and Crumbs in a short span of time, has become very popular among customers for customized couture cake designs. Cakes are created according to customers requirements, with a variety of flavors, in fondant and non-fondant designs. The also make custom made cake pops, cupcakes, cookies (sugar cookies, chip cookies etc..) and Candy table goodies such as tarts, brownies, macarons, truffles, cakesicles and all variety of desserts.

Their specialized expertise lies in birthday cakes and wedding cake decoration. And we make cakes for different occasions such as Reunion cake, Engagement cake, Brownie cake, Bridal shower cake, Different Festival cake and Mehendi Raat cake. With ample scope for creativity, they work around the theme to create bespoke creations that will be the cynosure of all eyes. Cakes n Crumbs has received a host of accolades and have been featured in leading cake decor websites, instagram and facebook in Kandy district and other district. They deliver all over Sri-Lanka.
